Privacy & Policy

Information We Collect

We collect personal information necessary for providing healthcare services, such as your name, contact details, medical history, insurance information, and payment details. Additionally, we may collect sensitive health information, including diagnosis, treatment plans, and medication history, to ensure appropriate care delivery. We also gather usage data, such as IP addresses and device information, to improve our services and enhance user experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to deliver healthcare services, personalize patient care, communicate appointment reminders, process insurance claims, and improve our medical services. Additionally, we may analyze aggregated data to identify trends, enhance medical research, and develop new treatment methods while ensuring patient confidentiality.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with healthcare professionals, insurance providers, and third-party service providers involved in delivering medical care, processing payments, and managing administrative tasks. Your data may also be disclosed in compliance with legal requirements, such as court orders or government regulations, to protect patient safety, prevent fraud, or respond to emergencies.

Data Retention

We retain your medical information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy and comply with legal, regulatory, and professional obligations. Upon request, we may securely delete or anonymize your data, subject to certain retention requirements mandated by healthcare regulations or legitimate business interests.

Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal and medical information from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include encryption, access controls, firewalls, regular security audits, and staff training on data protection protocols. Despite our efforts, no method of data transmission or storage can guarantee absolute security, and we continuously monitor and update our security practices to address emerging threats.

Your Rights

As a patient, you have certain rights regarding your medical information, including the right to access, correct, update, or request deletion of your records. You may also request restrictions on certain uses or disclosures of your data, receive copies of your medical records, and request confidential communication methods. If you have any concerns or questions about your privacy rights or wish to exercise these rights, please contact our Privacy Officer using the information provided below.

Cookie Usage

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this Privacy Policy and our Cookie Policy. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or opt-out of certain tracking mechanisms, although this may affect website functionality.

Data Protection Measures

We adhere to strict data protection standards and comply with applicable laws and regulations, such as the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our data protection measures include secure data storage, encryption of sensitive information, regular data backups, and staff training on privacy best practices. Additionally, we conduct periodic risk assessments and audits to identify and address potential vulnerabilities in our systems and processes.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or industry standards. Any revisions will be effective immediately upon posting on this page, and we encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for updates. Your continued use of our services after such changes constitute your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
