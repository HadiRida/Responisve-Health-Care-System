Cardiology Department

The Cardiology Department at Medical Center provides comprehensive cardiac care services, specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of various heart conditions.

Neurology Department

The Neurology Department offers expert diagnosis and treatment for a wide range of neurological disorders, including stroke, epilepsy, and Parkinson's disease.

Oncology Department

Our Oncology Department provides state-of-the-art cancer care, offering personalized treatment plans for various types of cancer, including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and immunotherapy.

Pediatrics Department

The Pediatrics Department specializes in providing compassionate and comprehensive medical care for infants, children, and adolescents, addressing a wide range of pediatric health issues.

Obstetrics & Gynecology Department

Our Obstetrics & Gynecology Department offers comprehensive care for women's health, including prenatal care, childbirth services, gynecological exams, and family planning.

Orthopedics Department

The Orthopedics Department provides advanced musculoskeletal care, including diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation for orthopedic conditions such as fractures, arthritis, and sports injuries.

Ear, Nose, and Throat (ENT) Department

The ENT Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders related to the ear, nose, throat, and related structures, offering surgical and non-surgical interventions.

Dermatology Department

Our Dermatology Department offers comprehensive skin care services, including diagnosis and treatment of skin conditions, cosmetic procedures, and skin cancer screenings.

Psychiatry Department

The Psychiatry Department provides compassionate mental health care, including diagnosis, therapy, and medication management for a wide range of psychiatric disorders.

Radiology Department

The Radiology Department offers advanced imaging services, including X-rays, MRI, CT scans, and ultrasound, to assist in the diagnosis and treatment of various medical conditions.
